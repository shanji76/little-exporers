Parent Questions

Frequently asked about Woodbridge Academy.

Quick answers to the things families ask most. Don't see your question? Email info@woodbridgeacademy-buford.com.

Where is Woodbridge Academy located?

Woodbridge Academy is located at 2466 Buford Highway, Duluth, GA 30097, serving families in Duluth, Johns Creek, Suwanee, and the greater Gwinnett County community.

What ages does Woodbridge Academy accept?

Our daycare and preschool programs serve children from six weeks through pre-K: Infants (6 weeks – 12 months), Toddlers (1 – 2 years), Preschool (3 – 4 years), and Pre-K (4 – 5 years). We also offer an after-school program for school-age children up to 12 years old.

What are your hours of operation?

Woodbridge Academy is open Monday through Friday, 6:30 AM to 6:30 PM. We are closed on weekends and federal holidays.

When does Woodbridge Academy open?

Woodbridge Academy opens soon. We are now booking private tours and accepting enrollment applications for our opening season.

How do I schedule a tour?

Private tours are by appointment, weekdays only. Email info@woodbridgeacademy-buford.com to schedule. We recommend booking early — opening-season spaces are limited.

Is Woodbridge Academy licensed?

Yes. Woodbridge Academy is a licensed early-learning academy in the State of Georgia with Bright from the Start — the Georgia Department of Early Care and Learning — with secure entry, low teacher-to-child ratios, background-checked staff, and CPR-certified educators on every shift.

Do you provide meals?

Yes. We provide fresh, chef-prepared breakfast, lunch, and snacks made on-site daily with seasonal ingredients.
